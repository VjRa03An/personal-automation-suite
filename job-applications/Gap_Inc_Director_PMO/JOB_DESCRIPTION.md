# Job Description — Director of PMO, Store Development
**Company:** Gap Inc.
**Location:** San Francisco, CA
**Posted:** April 2026
**URL:** https://www.gapinc.com/en-us/articles/2016/10/gap-inc-careers

## About the Role
The Director of PMO serves as the strategic integrator for Store Development — aligning vision, data, and cross-functional expertise to deliver an annual portfolio of capital investments.

## Key Responsibilities
- Lead end-to-end pipeline operating rhythm integrating milestones, risks, resources, and dependencies
- Synthesize complex operational and financial data into CFO/CEO/Board-level updates
- Drive structured decision-making across L1–L4 REC governance
- Oversee cross-functional program milestones across Creative, Design Development, Architecture, and Construction
- Create executive-ready presentations for VP+ audiences
- Independently lead complex, high-visibility initiatives

## Requirements
- 12+ years progressive experience in Program, Project, or Portfolio Management
- 6+ years leading PMO teams in complex, cross-functional environment
- Deep understanding of Store Development disciplines
- Advanced critical thinking and scenario modeling skills
- Strong executive communication — CFO/CEO/Board level
- Expertise in governance frameworks (REC L1–L4 or equivalent)

## Why Gap is Hiring This Role — My Read
1. They need a strategic integrator across Creative, Design, Architecture, Construction
2. Investment decisioning lacks clarity, rigor, and transparency (L1–L4 REC governance signal)
3. Store Development needs better CFO/CEO/Board-level executive visibility

## The Strategic Angle
Gap is making a deliberate bet on physical retail — not expansion, but capital discipline.
Athleta competing with Lululemon. Physical stores drive e-commerce in same market.
This role is about governing capital at the pace of their vision.
